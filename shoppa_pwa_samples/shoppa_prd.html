# Shoppa: Personal Shopper Operations Platform PRD ## 1. Project Overview Shoppa is a mobile-first Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for independent personal shoppers to manage multiple clients, shopping lists, and real-time operations efficiently. ## 2. User Roles * **Shopper (Primary):** Manages lists, shops in-store, tracks earnings. * **Client (Secondary):** Submits orders, tracks progress, approves price changes. ## 3. Core Features & User Flows ### Shopper Experience * **Dashboard:** High-level view of today's workload and earnings. * **Master Shopping List:** A unified view of all items across clients, grouped by category for efficient store navigation. Includes swipe actions for quick status updates. * **Allocation & Shortages:** Intelligent handling of partial availability with manual override. * **Delivery Management:** Packing assistance and status tracking. * **Analytics:** Detailed breakdown of profits and performance. * **Availability Management:** Controlling the "window" for receiving client orders. ### Client Experience * **Order Creation:** Flexible input (manual, text paste, image upload). * **Real-time Tracking:** Live progress bars and instant notification for price/availability conflicts. * **Messaging:** Quick-reply enabled chat for fast decisions. ## 4. Design Principles * **Clean & Modern:** Minimalist aesthetic (Uber + Notion + WhatsApp). * **Operational Efficiency:** One-handed use, large touch targets, 1-tap actions. * **Trust & Clarity:** Clear status indicators, real-time sync visibility, and offline-first indicators. ## 5. Screen Map 1. **Auth & Onboarding:** Signup/Login, Role Selection, Forgot Password. 2. **Shopper Home:** Dashboard, Active Sessions, Quick Actions. 3. **Master Shopping List:** The core operational view. 4. **Allocation Logic:** Handling shortages. 5. **Client List/Orders:** Management of individual client relationships. 6. **Analytics:** Earnings and summaries. 7. **Shopper Profile & Settings:** Availability, Price List, Categories. 8. **Client Home:** Active orders and status. 9. **Order Creation:** Multi-modal entry. 10. **Chat:** Real-time messaging. 11. **Delivery:** Pre-delivery packing and status updates. 12. **Feedback:** Post-delivery rating system.
