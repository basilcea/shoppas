1. Shopper & Client Responsibilities

Defining the professional relationship between users.

The Shopper Role

Shoppers act as independent contractors. You are responsible for accurate item selection, professional communication, and timely fulfillment of accepted orders. ShopOps does not provide tools or direct supervision of shopping activities.

Clients are required to provide clear, actionable shopping lists and accurate delivery instructions. By placing an order, you verify that you have the legal right to purchase the requested items and possess the funds required for the transaction.

3. Account Termination

Protocols for ending service access.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate our safety policies or engage in fraudulent activities. Termination can occur with or without prior notice at the sole discretion of ShopOps management.

Voluntary Closure

Users may close their accounts at any time via the Privacy Dashboard. Remaining balances will be cleared within 30 business days.

Involuntary Suspension

Accounts with ratings below 3.5 stars or those with repeated delivery disputes are subject to automated review and potential deactivation.
