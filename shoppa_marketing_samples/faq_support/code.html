A Shop Session is a dedicated time window where your personal shopper is exclusively focused on your order. Unlike standard delivery services, Shop Sessions are active, real-time collaborations. You'll receive a notification when your shopper starts, and they will communicate directly with you from the store floor via our PWA.

Substitutions are handled in real-time through the ShopOps digital concierge. If an item is out of stock, your shopper will:

  • check_circle Upload a photo of nearby available alternatives.
  • check_circle Suggest the closest match based on price and brand preference.
  • check_circle Wait for your approval before adding the replacement to the cart.

We use PWA technology (Progressive Web App).

Instead of downloading from an app store, you can "Add to Home Screen" directly from your browser. This gives you a full-screen, native experience including push notifications and offline access without the heavy battery drain or storage requirements of a traditional app.

iOS Instructions

Tap the Share icon ios_share and select "Add to Home Screen".

Android Instructions

Tap the three dots more_vert and select "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen".
